# Operational Data Analyst | Turning data into better business decisions

I work on practical analytics problems where data needs to lead to action. My projects focus on operations, demand, pricing, performance and customer behaviour, with a strong interest in building simple systems that help teams make better decisions.

## About Me

I analyse operational and commercial data to answer questions that matter to the business: what is driving demand, where efficiency is being lost, which actions improve outcomes, and how teams can respond faster with better information.

My work sits at the intersection of **data analysis**, **decision support**, and **systems thinking**.

## Featured Projects

### Predicting Demand for Perishable Goods
**Business problem:** Retailers need to balance product availability with the risk of waste on short shelf-life items.  
**Solution:** Analysed sales, pricing, promotions, weather and wastage data to uncover demand patterns and support better inventory planning.  
**Tools:** Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Jupyter

### Dynamic Pricing Engine
**Business problem:** Poor demand planning and static pricing can reduce margins and increase waste.  
**Solution:** Built a simple decision engine that combines demand signals and wastage patterns to recommend pricing or supply actions.  
**Tools:** Python, Pandas, SQLite, Airtable API

### Twilio Impact on Business Operations
**Business problem:** Credit control teams need to reduce low-value calls and improve time spent on revenue-generating activity.  
**Solution:** Used operational call data and KPI analysis to assess whether Twilio improved efficiency, call outcomes and potential time recovery.  
**Tools:** Excel, Pivot Tables, KPI analysis, dashboard design

### Ecommerce Cohort Analysis
**Business problem:** E-commerce teams need to understand whether customers return after their first purchase.  
**Solution:** Performed cohort analysis to measure retention patterns and compare customer behaviour over time.  
**Tools:** Python, Pandas, Seaborn, Jupyter

### Medoptix Healsight
**Business problem:** Healthcare operations need better visibility into admissions, flow and forecasting.  
**Solution:** Built a structured analytics pipeline covering data extraction, SQL validation, dataset building and predictive modelling.  
**Tools:** Python, SQL, Pandas, Matplotlib, Jupyter
